// current work

Research Focus

My PhD at Hasselt University centres on building a living evidence ecosystem for carbon pricing policy evaluation.

Meta-analysis

Carbon Pricing & Technological Innovation

Quantifying the causal impact of carbon pricing on innovation through harmonised evidence synthesis and meta-regression.

Systematic Review

Public Acceptability of Carbon Pricing

Synthesising evidence on the determinants of public support for carbon pricing instruments across contexts, policy designs, and socio-economic conditions.

Living Evidence

Ecosystem of Reviews

Building ML-assisted workflows for continuous evidence updates, codebook design, and cross-team data extraction coordination at carbonpricing.science.

Earlier work

Efficiency in Higher Education & Agriculture

Bootstrap DEA and conditional efficiency analysis of Irish public universities; econometric modelling for agricultural development and energy transition policy (GIZ, UNOPS).
